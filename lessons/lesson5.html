Exposure Systems and Light Sources

Exposure Systems and Light Sources
Exposure systems are critical in photolithography, and they use various light sources to expose patterns on the wafer. Step 1: Light Sources
Light sources, such as lasers and ultraviolet lamps, emit light of specific wavelengths required for the lithography process. Step 2: Collimation
The light is collimated and directed towards the photomask. Step 3: Mask Illumination
The collimated light passes through the photomask, which contains the pattern to be transferred. Step 4: Projection and Exposure
The pattern is projected onto the wafer, exposing the photoresist and initiating the pattern transfer process. Applications
